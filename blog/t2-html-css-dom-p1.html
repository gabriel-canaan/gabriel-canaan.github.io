definitions for my mum

  • html

    The pattern , the cut and fit of the dress.The placement of button holes, the length of zips

  • css

    The 'style'.That is the colours and textures and the type of fabric

  • dom

    It's the method and rule of reading and interpreting the "pattern" so as to present them in the correct 'fashion'. It provides the instructions on how to read the html to arrange the tree-like structure of a sites layout. starting at a root and dividing off into trunks and branches and so on.

  • box

    • boxify

      "A technical term" for deliniating a page structure.Throwing a rigid grid-like net around a design whereby the net surrounds all individual items and creates a rectangular "box" encompasing them and defining their place in the page structure itself.


    • box model

      • content

        The content of the box, where text and images appear

      • padding

        Clears an area around the content. The padding is transparent

      • border

        A border that goes around the padding and content

      • margin

        Clears an area outside the border. The margin is transparent
