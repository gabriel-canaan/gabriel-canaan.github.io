Compassionate leadership.

I have experienced the whole gambit of leadership styles, from overly friendly and casual through to draconian. I have also been on the other side many times. I’d like to think that I was not the former or the later, though I’m not sure all of my past employees would agree. What is abundantly clear to me is that a firm but fair, and consistent, approach is the one that gets the best results. I have worked for employers for whom crews would have walked thorough walls, and also for angry shouty ones whose walls were in no danger.
